OUR MISSION

EMPOWERING COMPLIANCE THROUGH INNOVATION

At AIGENTIC Compliance, we empower organizations and employees in integrity and compliance. Our AI-driven platform delivers immersive dilemma-based training and interactive compliance content within a virtual workspace.

THE CHALLENGE

  • Compliance is expensive, inefficient, and employees struggle to navigate policies.
  • Compliance officers face increasing complexity when implementing and monitoring regulatory requirements.
  • Employees don't recognize compliance risks, leading to costly breaches.
  • Traditional compliance training is ineffective and rarely retained.
  • Companies lack visibility into the effectiveness of their compliance programs.

MEET CLAUDIA

Through agentic AI and machine learning technology, our virtual compliance agent Claudia provides a solution to convert complicated laws and regulations in your organization's policies into Rule Sets that we use to develop training and deploy a virtual help desk.

Employees will have access to compliance guidance on-the-go, 24/7! Claudia can answer all questions employees may have on policies and guide them through the right process.

All their steps are documented, available in a dashboard and will be fed into the system again to ensure continuous improvement in functionality and user experience.

WHY IT WORKS

  • Employees receive instant, personalized compliance support and user-centric/relevant training.
  • End-to-end: Registration of compliance obligations, authorization flows and dashboarding are included.
  • Pro-active assistance and training triggered by events as to remind users of their compliance obligations.
  • Compliance officers get real-time insights into risk areas and benchmarked data, through dashboards, and reports.
  • Companies can reduce compliance failures and costs.
